# Marketing

## Scope

- Copy for landing pages, CTAs, headlines and descriptions
- Marketing emails, sequences and automations
- Ad copy (Google, Meta, etc.) and SEO
- Voice, persona and brand guidelines

## When documenting context

- Indicate the project's voice and brand guide, if any
- Reference where reference texts or glossary are
- Mention constraints (length, compliance, language)

## When generating rules

- Include references to copy guide or approved examples
- Suggest copy validation with owner when applicable
